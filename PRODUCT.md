# Think Beyond Tax

## Product

Think Beyond Tax is the public website for a collaborative identity used by independent UAE-based accounting, bookkeeping and tax professionals. It helps small and medium businesses understand available support, see how work fits their existing software, and start a direct requirements discussion.

## Audience

- UAE founders, owners and finance teams that need practical accounting or bookkeeping support.
- Businesses preparing VAT, Corporate Tax, EmaraTax or e-invoicing work.
- Teams with current records, backlogs, migrations or data-quality problems across common accounting systems.

## Promise

Experienced, named professionals provide practical support inside the systems a client already uses. Content should make scope, process and professional boundaries clear without overstating affiliations, authority or outcomes.

## Core destinations

- Accounting
- Bookkeeping
- VAT
- Corporate Tax
- E-Invoicing
- EmaraTax Support

## Trust requirements

- Think Beyond Tax is not an incorporated company, licensed firm, tax agency, FTA representative or accredited service provider.
- The responsible professional or licensed provider is named before paid work begins.
- Software familiarity does not imply certification, endorsement or guaranteed compatibility.
- Regulated claims must link to current official UAE sources and show a review date.
- Penalty waivers and similar applications are eligibility-based and never guaranteed.

## Conversion goal

Help a visitor identify the right service, understand the likely workflow, and choose to discuss their requirements by WhatsApp, email or the contact form.
